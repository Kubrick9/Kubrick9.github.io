Eureka!

Build the first multimodal agent-based video generation pipeline through 3D engine scripting.

How it works

Given any text prompt, multimodal agents collaborate to produce detailed Blender scripts to generate video with plausible character and motion consistency in any length.

Demo

Input Prompt: Show Spider-Man jumping off a skyscraper in a metropolitan city at night, emphasizing his bold descent against the city lights.
Input Prompt: Show a wounded female knight faltering and dramatically collapsing in a poisonous forest.
Input Prompt: Show D.Va relaxing in her bedroom, sitting down to snack and play video games.
Input Prompt: Show Iron Man swiftly jumping over a modern city at night, emphasizing his advanced physical capabilities and the city's nighttime glow.
